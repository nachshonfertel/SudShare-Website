FAQ

What are my hours and work area?

That’s up to you. This is your business. You’re the boss. You set your hours. Work when you want. You decide your work area. Take off when you feel like it.

How quickly does the laundry have to be done?

Customers are guaranteed next day delivery for orders in by noon, so you have 24-48 hours to complete an order.

How much money do I need to invest?

Nothing! Zero! Zilch. Assuming you have what’s required to start (which most people do), there is no investment required.

What do I need to start?

  • U.S. Social Security Number (for background check)
  • Access to a washer & dryer
  • Access to a drying rack, clothesline, or something to air-dry clothes
  • Laundry Detergent
  • A car or some way to pick-up and deliver
  • Scale

How much money can I make?

Most Sudsters earn $10-$20/hour. It depends on how efficient you work.
Your hourly and total monthly earnings depend on several factors.
  • Demand. The demand for wash-dry-fold services in your area affects job availability.
  • The size of your work area. You set your work area. The larger the area the more jobs you’ll get and the more money you make.
  • Days your available. The sudshare platform is available 24/7/365. The more days you’re available the more money you can make.
  • Your rating. All washers get a rating. The higher your rating the more jobs you’re offered. Your rating is determined mostly by customer feedback.
It’s impossible to predict exactly how much you’ll earn monthly, however, successful hard working washers in high-demand areas can earn up to $2500/month.
